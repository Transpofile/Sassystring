🌴 SASSY STRINGS — PRODUCT PITCH SHEET

Handmade Bead Accessories Inspired by Island Life

🌟 About Sassy Strings

Born from salty air, sun-kissed skin, and long walks along the shores of Siargao, Sassy Strings was created by a Filipina with a heart full of island dreams and creativity.

As a spiritually grounded woman who values beauty in simplicity, I started crafting bead accessories as a way to express my love for art, color, and joyful connection.

Each bracelet I make is more than an accessory—it’s a tiny story of the sea, a reflection of my roots, and a celebration of the vibrant, tropical life we’re blessed to live.

✨ Handmade with purpose. Designed with soul. Inspired by the islands.

🌺 COLLECTION HIGHLIGHTS

🌴 Island Charm
A true slice of paradise—this bracelet features a delicate mix of ocean-toned beads and authentic shell accents. It captures the calm, carefree vibe of Siargao sunsets and salty air.

🌊 Ocean Breeze Twist
Braided like the waves and infused with sea spirit. Blends seafoam greens, aquas, and blues—perfect for free spirits and ocean chasers.

💖 Sunset Stackables (3-in-1)
Three bracelets, one bold tropical statement. Warm beachy tones—corals, golds, twilight pinks. Wear together or mix & match.

📿 Personalized Name Bracelets
Spell your story! Custom name, location, or island words. A meaningful, wearable souvenir.

💎 Gemstone Touch
Features semi-precious stones like turquoise, lava rock, or quartz—each chosen for its natural island energy.

🌾 Native Inspired
Uses traditional Filipino patterns, wooden beads, and earthy tones—rooted in heritage.

🌈 Vibrant Colors Collection
Bright and joyful! Designed for beach parties, fiestas, and playful island souls.

🐚 Pearl in Strings
A dainty beach classic. Delicate pearls woven into colorful thread—elegant, breezy, and tropical.
